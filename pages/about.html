About Decoding Disinformation

Every year, millions fall prey to various types of misinformation and disinformation featured on the internet, and with the rise of AI, scams, fake information, and the impersonation of individuals has only gotten easier to create and harder to detect. For these reasons, our team wanted to find a solution to this ever-evolving problem, or at the very least, a means to prevent our most vulnerable from becoming victims of the misinformation epidemic.

There is a wide variety of misinformation and disinformation on the internet. Examples include fake news articles, social media manipulation, phishing, and with the advent of AI; AI voice filters and deepfakes. Every demographic is susceptible to online misinformation as it becomes more prevalent and difficult to identify. Through our website, we will provide tips, tricks, guides, and examples, and refer to other resources to help users identify common scams and how to avoid them.

Our website aims to assist people from all walks of life in identifying misinformation and disinformation, guide them in investigating any content they may be suspicious of, and identify and utilize tips and tricks to maintain healthy internet practices and protect themselves from misleading content.
